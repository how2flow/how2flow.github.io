### Steve Jeong (how2flow)
<br>

Hello! I am **Steve Jeong**, an embedded software engineer.<br>
My nickname, **how2flow**, asks the question: *"How should data and logic flow?"*<br>
It reflects my drive to design efficient drivers, robust system architectures, and optimal workflows.<br>
I constantly seek the best "flow" to evolve as a developer.

#### 🔧 Technical Focus
My core expertise lies in **Board Support Package (BSP)** and **Linux Kernel Development**.<br>
I specialize in bringing up various **System-on-Chip (SoC)** platforms and integrating hardware peripherals.<br>
My academic background is in **Control Engineering**, which gives me a strong foundation in understanding hardware-software interactions.

#### 📝 Blog Structure
I categorize my content to share knowledge and technical insights effectively:
*   **Posts**: In-depth articles covering **Linux Kernel**, **RTOS**, **Device Drivers**, and **System Architecture**.
*   **Documents**: A space reserved for future technical archives and extensive manuals.
*   **Diary**: Casual updates on my daily life, reading records, and personal thoughts.

#### 🚀 Goals & Interests
I am dedicated to becoming a world-class **Linux System Programmer**.<br>
I believe in continuous learning, open-source contribution, and the power of sharing knowledge.<br>
Feel free to reach out to discuss system programming, embedded Linux, or kernel architectures!
